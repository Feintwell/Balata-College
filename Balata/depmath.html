What We Teach

Math

The Mathematics curriculum in Fiji covers a wide range of topics designed to build foundational and advanced skills. At [School Name], students are guided through:

  • Arithmetic
    Mastering number operations, percentages, ratios, and financial literacy.
  • Algebra
    Understanding patterns, solving equations, and working with expressions.
  • Geometry
    Exploring shapes, angles, measurements, and spatial reasoning.
  • Statistics
    Collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data for informed decision-making.
  • Basic Calculus
    Introducing concepts of change, motion, and rates for senior-level students.

Physics

Physics is a core subject offered under our Mathematics Department, designed to develop students' scientific thinking and understanding of the physical world. At [School Name], we follow the Fijian national curriculum, which provides a balanced foundation in classical and modern physics, with a strong emphasis on real-world applications and practical investigations.

  • Mechanics
    Study of motion, forces, energy, and momentum in real-world and experimental contexts.
  • Thermodynamics
    Exploration of heat, temperature, energy transfer, and the laws governing thermodynamic systems.
  • Electromagnetism
    Exploring shapes, angles, measurements, and spatial reasoning.
  • Waves and Optics
    Investigation of wave properties, sound, light behavior, and the use of optical instruments.
  • Modern Physics
    Introduction to advanced concepts such as quantum mechanics, atomic structure, and relativity.
