Welcome to Enrollment

Thank you for considering Balata College. We welcome new students each year and look forward to guiding you through the enrollment process.

Enrollment Requirements

  • Completed Application Form
  • Certified Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Two Passport-Size Photos
  • Past Academic Reports
  • Immunization/Vaccination Record
  • Transfer Letter (if applicable)

How to Enroll

  • Collect and submit the provisional application form.
  • Attend an interview and assessment session.
  • Await admission confirmation from the college.
  • Submit official application with final documents.
  • Expatriate students must make fee payments.

Why Choose Us?

We provide a holistic educational experience through a balance of academic, cultural, and technical subjects. Our dedicated teachers and modern facilities ensure students are prepared for the future.
