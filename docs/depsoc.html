Social Science Department

Welcome to the Social Science Department at Balata College, where we inspire students to explore the complexities of human societies, cultures, and environments. Our curriculum is designed to cultivate critical thinking, cultural awareness, and a deep understanding of the world around us.

Our Offerings

We provide a comprehensive curriculum that includes:

  • Social Science (Years 9–10)
  • Geography (Years 11–13)
  • History (Years 11–13)

These subjects equip students with the knowledge and skills to analyze societal issues, understand historical contexts, and appreciate diverse cultures and environments.
