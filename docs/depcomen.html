Commerce Department

Welcome to the Commerce Department of Nadi Muslim College, where we prepare students for success in the dynamic world of business and economics. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to fostering knowledge, skills, and values that empower students to thrive in their academic and professional pursuits.

Overview

We offer a comprehensive curriculum aligned with the Ministry of Education's standards, including:

  • Commercial Studies
  • Accounting
  • Economics

These subjects provide students with a solid foundation in understanding trade, finance, and industry, equipping them with practical skills for real-world applications.

Our Goals

  • Impart Knowledge:
    Equip students with essential commerce knowledge to foster rational thinking and prepare them for productive citizenship.
  • Foundation for Higher Education:
    Provide a strong base for students aspiring to pursue tertiary education in universities.
  • Develop Desirable Qualities:
    Encourage self-reliance, creativity, quick decision-making, and innovation, promoting all-round development.
