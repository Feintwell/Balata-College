Science Department

Welcome to the Science Department at Nadi Muslim College, where we ignite curiosity and foster a deep understanding of the natural world. Our department offers a robust curriculum in Biology, Chemistry, and Physics, aimed at developing critical thinking, analytical skills, and a passion for scientific inquiry.

Our Goals

  • Basic Science
    General knowlegde of science and it's foundation.
  • Chemistry
    Exploration of substances, their properties, reactions, and the use of such reactions to form new substances.
  • Biology
    Study of living organisms, their structure, function, growth, and evolution.
