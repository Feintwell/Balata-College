PEMAC Department

Welcome to the PEMAC (Physical Education, Music, Arts, and Culture) Department at Balata College. Our mission is to foster holistic development in students by offering a diverse and enriching curriculum that spans physical education, music, visual arts, and cultural studies. We aim to develop both the physical and creative capacities of students, helping them become well-rounded individuals skilled in sports, physical activities, and artistic and cultural appreciation.

Our Mission

To provide students with a comprehensive education that nurtures their physical, artistic, and cultural development, empowering them to lead healthy, creative, and balanced lives. Through a variety of programs and activities, we aim to instill in students a sense of discipline, creativity, and cultural awareness.

Our Offerings

The PEMAC Department offers a wide range of subjects and activities across various strands that allow students to explore their interests and develop their talents:

  • Physical Education
    Our physical education program promotes physical fitness, health, and well-being. Students engage in various sports, fitness routines, and outdoor activities that help them develop teamwork, leadership, and personal discipline.
  • Music
    The music curriculum introduces students to various musical forms, instruments, and techniques, encouraging creativity and expression through sound. Students learn to appreciate different genres of music while gaining the skills to perform individually or as part of an ensemble.
  • Visual Arts
    The visual arts program explores various art forms, including drawing, painting, sculpture, and digital media. Students are encouraged to develop their artistic skills and use their creativity to express their ideas and emotions visually.
  • Cultural Studies
    In the cultural studies component, students learn to appreciate and understand the diverse cultures that make up Fiji and the wider world. This includes an exploration of traditions, customs, and art forms from different cultural backgrounds.
